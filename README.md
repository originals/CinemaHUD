## Features

**Twitch integration** — watch live Twitch streams with quality selection

**Custom video URLs** — play any video URL supported by LibVLC (MP4, AVI, MKV, WebM, local files, streaming URLs, M3U8 playlists, internet radio, and more)

**Two display modes**:
  - **On-Screen** — floating window overlay you can freely position and resize
  - **ingame World** — place video screens at custom locations in the game world

**Add and save** — Add and save your own streams and world locations

**Playback controls** — play, pause, volume control, twitch chat, settings and close/disable buttons

**Quality selection** — choose stream quality for twitch streams



## Display Modes

### On-Screen Display

A floating video window that stays on top of the game. Drag to reposition and resize to your preference.

### ingame World Display

Place a virtual screen at any location in the game world. The screen renders in 3D and responds to your camera position.

  <img height="400" alt="ingame World Display Example" src="https://www.gw2opus.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/ingame-example.png" />

## Known Limitations

**In-game world screens** are always rendered on top. players and objects positioned between you and the screen will not occlude it.
